IT'S BACK TO SCHOOL TIME FOR THE PARENTS.

Talkative Parents is a revolutionary communication application (it's the only one of its kind) dedicated solely to the parents of school going kids. It is packed with features that make interacting with teachers, principals and other parents easier.

  • Get up-to-the-minute updates from your child's school

  • It doesn't matter where you're from. Multiple schools from across the globe

  • Chat with parents of children from your child's class

  • Receive notices from your child's school, direct to your phone

  • Pay tuition fees through our secure payment gateway*
